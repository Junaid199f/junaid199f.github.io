I am a third-year PhD research scholar at the IRIMAS Lab, Universite de Haute Alsace, located in Mulhouse City, France's Haut-Rhin department. My thesis topic is "Automated Machine Learning with Metaheuristics." During my thesis, I have proposed multiple AutoML approaches utilizing evolutionary algorithms for multiple medical image analysis tasks, e.g., medical image classification, medical image segmentation, etc. 

I have over half a decade of experience in building Web based ML applications for different medical imaging tasks.
